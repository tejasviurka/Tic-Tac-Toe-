Rules of Tic Tac Toe

×

1. The game is played on a 3x3 grid.

2. Players take turns to place their marker (X or O) on an empty square.

3. The first player to get three of their markers in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) winsthe game.

4. If all nine spots are filled and neither player has won, the game is considered a draw.
